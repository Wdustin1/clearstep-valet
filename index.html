How It Works

Simple as setting it outside.

No scheduling. No apps. No trips to the dumpster. Just leave it at your door and it's gone by morning.

01
Set It Outside Your Door

Each unit gets a provided 13-gallon trash bin. Between 7–10 PM, just set your bag in or beside it outside your door. That's it.

02
We Collect Overnight

Our uniformed, background-checked team walks every hallway between 8 PM and midnight — collecting bags quietly and efficiently.

03
Wake Up to a Clean Hallway

Everything goes to the community dumpster or compactor — no mess, no smells, no overflowing bins. Done before sunrise.
