Executive Summary

Valet Property Solutions (VPS) provides doorstep valet trash pickup for apartment communities. Our valets collect trash from outside each resident's door 5–7 nights per week and haul it to the on-site dumpster — a hands-off, premium amenity that residents love and property managers profit from.

Valet trash is the #1 rated resident amenity in multifamily housing nationally, yet no established local provider currently serves the Wichita market. National companies focus on larger metros and charge higher rates. VPS fills that gap as a locally owned, competitively priced alternative with the technology, branding, and operational plan ready to launch.

We are seeking $5,000 in startup funding to purchase equipment and launch marketing. The business generates revenue from Month 1 of service, and we project full repayment within 90–120 days of landing our first property contract.

Target Properties

Apartment communities with 75–300+ units in the Wichita metro area — Wichita, Derby, Andover, Maize, and Haysville.

Class B and C properties are our sweet spot. These managers feel the most pressure to add revenue without raising base rent. The NOI pitch lands hardest here.

Property managers currently have two options: let residents self-manage trash (leading to overflows and complaints), or pay a national provider who doesn't prioritize their market.

VPS is the local alternative — owner-operated, competitively priced, accountable, and backed by purpose-built technology.

Operations

Nightly Routes

Residents place trash in bins outside their doors by 9 PM. Valets walk every hallway between 8 PM–midnight, collecting and hauling to the community dumpster. Each valet handles 100–150 units per shift (~2 hours). Route completion is logged in the VPS app with unit-level tracking.

Scaling

Owner-operated through 200–300 units (1–2 properties). First hire at 300+ units — part-time evening positions, $13–16/hour. Each additional employee adds ~150 units of capacity.

Quality Control

Nightly digital route reports auto-generated and shareable with property managers. Unit-level issue logging with photo documentation. Direct messaging between admin and valets through the app. Same-day response on property manager concerns.
