Executive Summary

Valet Property Solutions (VPS) provides doorstep valet trash pickup for apartment communities. Our valets collect trash from outside each resident's door 5–7 nights per week and haul it to the on-site dumpster — a hands-off, premium amenity that residents love and property managers profit from.

Valet trash is the #1 rated resident amenity in multifamily housing nationally, yet no established local provider currently serves the Wichita market. National companies focus on larger metros and charge higher rates. VPS fills that gap as a locally owned, competitively priced alternative ready to launch.

We are seeking $5,000 in startup funding to purchase equipment and launch marketing. Revenue begins Month 1 of service, with full repayment projected within 90–120 days of our first contract.

Operations

Nightly Routes

Residents place trash in bins by 9 PM. Valets walk every hallway 8 PM–midnight, collecting and hauling to dumpster. Each valet handles 100–150 units (~2 hours). Route completion logged in app with unit-level tracking.

Scaling

Owner-operated through 200–300 units. First hire at 300+ — part-time evenings, $13–16/hr. Each employee adds ~150 units of capacity.

Quality Control

Auto-generated nightly reports shareable with PMs. Unit-level issue logging with photos. Direct admin↔valet messaging. Same-day response SLA.
