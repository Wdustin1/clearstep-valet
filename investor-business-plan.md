## Executive Summary

Valet Property Solutions (VPS) provides doorstep valet trash pickup for apartment communities. Our valets collect trash from outside each resident's door 5–7 nights per week and haul it to the on-site dumpster — a hands-off, premium amenity that residents love and property managers profit from.

Valet trash is the **#1 rated resident amenity** in multifamily housing nationally, yet no established local provider currently serves the Wichita market. National companies focus on larger metros and charge higher rates. VPS fills that gap as a locally owned, competitively priced alternative with the technology, branding, and operational plan ready to launch.

**We are seeking $5,000 in startup funding** to purchase initial equipment and launch marketing. The business model generates revenue from Month 1 of service, and we project full repayment within 90–120 days of landing our first property contract.

## How the Business Works

The model is simple and the math is straightforward.

**VPS charges the property manager $35 per unit per month.**
The property manager passes the cost to residents as a line item — typically $50/month.
The property manager keeps $15/unit/month as pure profit. No out-of-pocket cost to them.

This is why properties say yes: **we hand them a new revenue stream they didn't have before.**

| | Per Unit/Month |
|---|---|
| VPS charges property | $35 |
| Property charges resident | $50 |
| **Property manager net profit** | **$15** |

### What VPS Provides
- Doorstep trash pickup 5–7 nights/week on a set schedule
- Small collection bin placed outside each unit door (included)
- Background-checked, uniformed valets
- Nightly route completion reports via our custom-built management app
- General liability insurance with COI naming the property as additional insured
- One local point of contact — not a national call center

## Operations

### Nightly Route
- Residents place trash in bins outside their doors by 9 PM
- Valets walk every hallway between 8 PM–midnight, collecting and hauling to the community dumpster
- Each valet handles 100–150 units per shift (~2 hours)
- Route completion is logged in the VPS app with unit-level tracking

### Scaling
- Owner-operated through 200–300 units (1–2 properties)
- First hire at 300+ units
- Part-time evening positions, $13–16/hour depending on route size
- Each additional employee adds ~150 units of capacity

### Quality Control
- Nightly digital route reports (auto-generated, shareable with property managers)
- Unit-level issue logging with photo documentation
- Direct messaging between admin and valets through the app
- Same-day response SLA on property manager concerns
