How It Works — 3 Steps for Residents
STEP 01
Set it outside the door
Residents place their trash bag in the provided bin outside their door between 7–10 PM. No trips to the dumpster.
STEP 02
We collect overnight
Our team walks every hallway between 8 PM–midnight, collecting quietly and professionally. Done before sunrise.
STEP 03
Clean hallway by morning
Everything goes to the community dumpster or compactor. No mess, no odors, no overflowing bins. Residents wake up to a clean hallway.
